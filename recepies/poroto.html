POROTA

Decsription

Porota (also known as paratha or parantha) is a type of flatbread that is popular in South Asian cuisine,

particularly in India, Pakistan, and Bangladesh Porota can be served plain or stuffed with a variety of fillings,

such as spiced potatoes, onions, paneer (Indian cottage cheese), or minced meat. The filling is typically placed in the center of

 
the rolled-out dough, which is then folded and rolled out again to seal the filling inside. 
The porota is then cooked on a griddle until it is golden brown and slightly crispy.
